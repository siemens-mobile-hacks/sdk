◆ OS_TICK_PERIOD_US

#define OS_TICK_PERIOD_US   4615

Duration of one OS tick in microseconds.

Defines the period of a single system tick. All conversions between ticks and real time are based on this value.

1 tick = 4.615 ms = 4615 µs
